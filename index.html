Our Story

Rooted in Discipline, Growing with Nature

Born from the values of dedication and service, Fauji Agro Enterprise was founded with a mission to bring high-quality, affordable organic fertilizers to every farmer in India.

  • We use scientifically managed vermiculture beds with selected Eisenia fetida earthworms for superior compost quality.
  • Our farm follows strict organic protocols — no chemicals, no shortcuts, only pure natural composting.
  • We support small and marginal farmers with bulk deals and doorstep delivery across the region.
  • Every batch is tested for nutrient content, ensuring consistent quality you can trust.
Why Vermicompost

The Superfood for Your Soil

Vermicompost is far superior to chemical fertilizers and regular compost. It improves soil health, boosts plant immunity, and dramatically increases crop yield — naturally.

🌾

Higher Crop Yield

Rich in NPK and micro-nutrients that are readily available to plants, leading to up to 40% better yields compared to chemical fertilizers.

💧

Better Water Retention

Improves soil porosity and water-holding capacity, reducing irrigation frequency and helping crops survive dry spells.

🦠

Healthy Soil Microbiome

Packed with beneficial microorganisms that improve soil structure, fight disease, and accelerate nutrient cycling.

☣️

Zero Chemical Residue

100% natural product. Safe for your family, your livestock, groundwater, and the surrounding ecosystem.

💪

Plant Disease Resistance

The humic acids and enzymes in vermicompost strengthen root systems and boost natural plant immunity.

♻️

Eco-Friendly Farming

Using vermicompost reduces your carbon footprint, revives degraded soils, and contributes to a sustainable future.
